					<div class="box light guide no-top-border" style="padding: 10px;">
						This is the one trophy that will decide whether you do or don't get the platinum trophy for DiRT Rally 2.0.<div><br /></div>To earn this trophy, you must:<ol><li>Buy the <a href="https://dirt.fandom.com/wiki/Audi_Sport_quattro_S1_E2">Audi Sport quattro S1 E2</a>. this will cost 200,000 - 250,000 credits depending on if you get a new or used variation from the classifieds.</li><li>You need to be in the top tier of the leaderboards (The top 10%) for any Group B daily challenge using the Audi. This will vary depending on your skill and knowledge of the track and car, as well as how many people log into DiRT and complete the stage on any given day.</li></ol><div><br /></div>Daily challenges also offer a sense of difficulty as once you enter the event, you have one shot and nothing else. <ul><li>There is no restart option.</li><li>Quitting to main menu or retiring from event at any time will count as a DNF, and closing application mid-stage gives you the maximum amount of time for that stage (Either 15 or 30 minutes), while holding your car until the event runs out.</li><li>You do not get to see the exact stage you are driving until you enter the event, however you can see the location and class of car. The leaderboards cannot be viewed until completion of the stage.</li><li>It is better to be slightly cautious and smooth with the car than it is to try and set a blistering pace and end up with an irretrievable or terminally damaged car. The bottom tier of the leaderboards are always people who have quit the stage or terminally damaged their car, so you are guaranteed around the second and third tier even with some mistakes.</li></ul><br />However don't freak out as there are ways to get around this and make it easier for yourself.<br /><div><br /></div>It will help to use the car as much as possible once you get it as you will be able to purchase upgrades over time, allowing you to be faster, have a more reliable car, and unlock the full range of tuning options to tweak. If you are looking for guides on how to setup your car, <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RuestCK6kog&list=PLcbXT3zPpPUj2uz3zH-BOz8xoke9AsEw4" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">here</a> is a playlist by YouTuber <em>SkyRex. </em>Included in the playlist is general guides for how to setup your cars for each base game and DLC location. These are helpful starting points for how you should approach a rally, but as all drivers have a different drive technique, you may need to experiment to create an optimum setup for your car.<br /><br />When you view the location, you should practice on a few different stages and warm-up in the car before going for your run. If you want to be extra sure of what stage it is, you can use a trick from the original DiRT Rally. This trick would be that some people would start the event up on a different account to see what stage it is and run the stage with that car, before practicing and then going for an attempt on the intended account.<br /><br />You can boost your attempts at this trophy by having the season pass, as this makes the bonus challenge events available to you. But if not, you should get at least 1 attempt at this trophy every week or two. Do not stress if you do not see one of these events every day as the developers try to rotate all locations and classes of car around as best as they can. <strong>Any Promo, Bonus or Delta events will count for this trophy,</strong>
<strong>Daily AI challenges do not count for this trophy, all weekly and monthly challenges do not either.</strong><div><br /></div>The Global reset for Daily challenges is at <strong>10:00am GMT</strong> time, you can also view where you are on the leaderboard and how long is left on the event if you sign in to your Racenet account at the official <a href="https://dirtrally2.com/community-events" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">Dirt Rally 2 Community Events</a> site. If the rewards screen does not come up instantly, try closing application and restarting the game, or do a community or career event to trigger it.<br /><br />Don't worry or be un-motivated by your first few attempts, some people may get lucky the first time, but many will be trying months to achieve this. Here is my <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7sLBVmQ04YM" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">live reaction</a> after a few months of attempts.<br /><div><br /></div>This trophy was buggy on launch as there was originally no passable objectives to unlock this trophy in the games code until V1.05, it also took Codemasters a month after launch to add any Group B daily challenge into the rotation.
